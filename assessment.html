"Making Education Great Again"

Education Syllabus is a site designed to target Sustainable Development Goal(SDG) 4 which is "Quality Education" this site is also designed to provide quality information about Education, it displays the syllabuses, topics, materials offered by Government secondary schools, Private secondary schools in Nigeria and Syllabus of schools outside Nigeria. this site also provides information on opportunities available for secondary school students, such as Admission notice to higher institutions, competitions, scholarships and many more. this site serves as an information ground for secondary school students, to inform them and to prepare them for a better future.
